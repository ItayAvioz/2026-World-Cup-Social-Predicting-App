# ⚽ WorldCup 2026 — Predict. Compete. Get Roasted.

> 48 teams. 104 matches. One champion.
> Who in your group actually knows football — and who just gets lucky?

---

## The Idea

You and your friends all think you know best. **Now prove it.**

Before every game, everyone submits their predicted scoreline. The moment the whistle blows, predictions lock and reveal — you see exactly what each person picked, who played it safe, and who thought they knew something nobody else did.

Points stack up over 6 weeks of World Cup football. One leaderboard. No excuses.

---

## How It Works

### 🎯 Before the Tournament
- Pick your **tournament champion** — which country lifts the trophy
- Pick the **top scorer** — who ends the tournament with the most goals
- Both picks lock at kickoff on **June 11**. No changing your mind.

### ⚡ During the Tournament
- Submit your **predicted scoreline** before every single game
- Predictions lock the moment kickoff happens — no last-second edits
- Points awarded automatically after the final whistle

### 🔓 At Kickoff — Predictions Revealed
The moment the game starts, all predictions go public. You'll see:
- How many people picked each team to win
- Who went for the upset
- The most popular scoreline
- Who went against the crowd — and whether it paid off

## 📊 Data That Helps You Predict

Before each game, the app shows you real tournament data for both teams so you can make smarter picks:

- Results so far in this tournament (W / D / L)
- Goals scored & goals conceded
- Cards collected (yellow & red)
- Corner stats
- Head-to-head prediction breakdown from your group vs the global pool

No more blind guessing — use the numbers.

---

## 👥 Your Group

This isn't a public leaderboard full of strangers. You compete in **your own private group**.

One person creates the group and sends a **WhatsApp invite link**. Friends click, sign up, and land straight inside the group. That's it — no codes to type, no manual joining.

- Each group has its own **leaderboard**
- Each group gets its own **nightly AI summary**
- You can be in **multiple groups**

---

## 🤖 Nightly AI Roast

Every night after the day's games, an AI writes a summary for your group.

Not a boring recap. A **roast**.

Who climbed the table, who fell off a cliff, who had the audacity to predict *that* scoreline — and got it exactly right (or embarrassingly wrong). Funny, sharp, and completely ruthless.
